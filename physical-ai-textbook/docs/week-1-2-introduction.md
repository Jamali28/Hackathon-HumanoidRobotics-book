# Weeks 1-2: Introduction to Physical AI, Sensors, and Humanoid Robotics Landscape

This introductory section sets the stage for the entire course, providing a broad overview of Physical AI, the role of sensors in robotics, and the exciting landscape of humanoid robotics.

## Learning Objectives

*   Understand the foundational concepts of Physical AI.
*   Explore different types of sensors used in robotics (e.g., LiDAR, IMU, cameras).
*   Familiarize with the current state and future trends of humanoid robotics.

## Topics Covered

*   What is Physical AI?
*   The importance of Embodied Intelligence
*   Sensor fundamentals and applications
*   Overview of humanoid robot platforms and their capabilities

## Lab Exercises

*   **Lab 1.1**: Sensor data visualization (basic data plotting).
*   **Lab 1.2**: Introduction to robot simulation environment (basic scene interaction).
