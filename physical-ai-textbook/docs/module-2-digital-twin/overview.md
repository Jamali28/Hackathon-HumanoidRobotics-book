# Module 2: Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity) - Overview

This module introduces the concept of digital twins in robotics, focusing on simulation environments like Gazebo and Unity. Digital twins are essential for testing and validating robot behaviors in a virtual space before deployment to physical hardware.

## Topics Covered

*   What is a Digital Twin?
*   Introduction to Gazebo Simulation
*   Introduction to Unity for Robotics Visualization
*   Benefits of Digital Twins in Physical AI
