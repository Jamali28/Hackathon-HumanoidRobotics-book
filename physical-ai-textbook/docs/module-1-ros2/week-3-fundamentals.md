# Module 1: ROS 2 - Week 3: ROS 2 Fundamentals

This week focuses on the core concepts of ROS 2, including its architecture, command-line tools, and basic communication mechanisms.

## Learning Objectives

*   Understand the ROS 2 graph and its components.
*   Learn to use ROS 2 command-line tools.
*   Implement basic ROS 2 publishers and subscribers.

## Lab Exercises

*   **Lab 3.1**: ROS 2 `talker` and `listener` implementation.
*   **Lab 3.2**: Inspecting the ROS 2 graph with `rqt_graph`.
